Venezuelan Christmas Recipes

Venezuelan gastronomy is the product of the European and African influence that took place during the colonizing era, a period in which the local indigenous people adapted various recipes using local ingredients and, as a result, creating dishes, desserts and drinks that nowadays they decorate tables during Christmas.

Main Dishes

  • Hallaca
  • Chicken Salad
  • Jam Bread
  • Pernil
